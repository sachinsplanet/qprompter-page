The short version: Qprompter stores everything locally in your browser. No data is sent to us, no accounts are required, and no personal information is collected. Your prompts are yours and stay on your device.
01

Overview

Qprompter ("Extension", "we", "us", "our") is a Chrome browser extension developed by Mr. Sachin. This Privacy Policy describes how the Extension handles information in connection with your use of it.

Our core design principle is local-first privacy: the Extension is architected to operate entirely within your browser. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal information. There is no account system, no sign-up, and no telemetry.

02

Data We Collect

Qprompter does not collect, transmit, or have access to any data from your use of the Extension. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your prompt text or queue contents
  • We do not receive any information about which AI platforms you use or how often
  • We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • We do not track identifiers such as IP addresses, device IDs, or browser fingerprints
  • We have no access to your Chrome account, Google account, or any account on third-party AI platforms

All operational data generated by your use of Qprompter is stored exclusively in your local browser storage (see Section 3).

03

Local Storage

To function, Qprompter stores data locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it. The following data is stored locally:

Storage API Data Stored Purpose
chrome.storage.local Prompt queue, template library, execution history, user settings Persists your queue and preferences across browser sessions
chrome.storage.session Processing locks, active queue state Manages active queue state; auto-cleared when Chrome closes
Your control: You can clear all locally stored Qprompter data at any time by using the Clear function within the Extension, by removing the Extension from Chrome, or by clearing your browser's extension storage through Chrome's settings.
04

How Data Is Used

Locally stored data is used solely to provide the Extension's core features:

  • Queue management — your prompt queue is stored so it persists between browser sessions and survives the Extension's background service worker sleeping
  • Template library — prompt templates you save are stored locally so you can reuse them in future sessions
  • Execution history — a log of submitted prompts is stored locally so you can review past queue runs
  • Settings — your configured preferences (gap intervals, Smart Waiter toggles, etc.) are stored locally so they persist between sessions

This data is never used for advertising, profiling, cross-site tracking, or any purpose other than operating the Extension's stated features.

05

What We Don't Collect

To be explicit, Qprompter does not collect or access:

  • The content of your prompts or any text you enter into AI platforms
  • The responses generated by AI platforms
  • Your browsing history or activity on websites other than where the Extension is active
  • Personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Payment information (the Extension is free)
  • Location data
  • Files, images, or other content from your device
  • Any data from your Chrome sync profile or Google Account
06

Third-Party Platforms

Qprompter automates interactions with third-party AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Copilot, Perplexity, DeepSeek, Mistral, and others). When you use the Extension on these platforms:

  • Your interactions with those platforms are subject to their privacy policies and terms of service, not ours
  • The content of your prompts is transmitted directly from your browser to those platforms — we do not intercept, view, or store this transmission
  • Those platforms may log, analyze, or use your prompts in accordance with their own policies
  • We have no control over how those platforms handle your data

The Extension also loads web fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This connection is made by your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's privacy policy. No Extension-specific or user data is included in this request beyond standard HTTP headers.

The Extension is distributed through the Chrome Web Store, which is operated by Google. Downloading, installing, and reviewing the Extension through the Chrome Web Store is subject to Google's privacy policy.

07

Browser Permissions

Qprompter requests the following Chrome permissions, used only for the purposes described:

  • activeTab / scripting — required to inject the prompt automation script into the active AI chat tab when you trigger a queue run. The Extension only reads and modifies the DOM of AI platform pages you are actively using.
  • storage — required to save your queue, templates, history, and settings locally in chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.session.
  • offscreen — required to maintain an invisible document that keeps the background service worker alive during queue execution, preventing Chrome from suspending it mid-run.
  • alarms — required to schedule timed delays between prompt submissions in the queue.

The Extension does not request access to all URLs, your browsing history, cookies, webcam, microphone, or any other sensitive browser capability beyond what is listed above.

08

Children's Privacy

Qprompter is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the Extension does not collect any personal information from any user, the risk of inadvertent collection from minors is minimal.

If you believe a child under 13 is using this Extension, please note that the Extension itself poses no data collection risk. However, the third-party AI platforms the Extension connects to may have their own age restrictions and parental guidance requirements.

09

Security

Because Qprompter does not transmit or store your data on any external server, the primary data security surface is your local browser storage. Chrome's extension storage APIs are sandboxed per-extension, meaning other extensions and websites cannot access Qprompter's stored data.

The security of your local data depends on the security of your device and Chrome browser. We recommend keeping Chrome and your operating system up to date. If your device is compromised, local browser storage may be accessible to malicious software — this is a general browser security consideration, not specific to Qprompter.

Open by design: The Extension's source code is distributed as a Chrome extension package. Technically inclined users can inspect the extension's scripts to verify the privacy claims made in this policy.
